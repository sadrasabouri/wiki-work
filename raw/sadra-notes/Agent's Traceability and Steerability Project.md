The issue of agent's action review very prominent. Users are the passive observers that meet the agents output at end of the execution road. This is not a good practice and the stakes are higher as these generated outputs becomes with more fidelity (for example spreadsheets). This paradigm would lead to user comparing the final output to their desired and re-prompt the whole process again. Therefor two main challenge exists: lack of traceability and steer-ability of agents in spreadsheets.
In a recent project we used slow-revealing mechanism to show the agent's actions step-by-step and give them ability to request for edit as going through these steps. These efforts lead to this research paper: [[Auditing and Controlling AI Agent Actions in Spreadsheets]]
