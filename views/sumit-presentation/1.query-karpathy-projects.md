## Known Limitations Across All Projects

Per Anatoly Krasnovsky's commentary, cited in [[LLM-Wiki Related Works]]:

1. **Lossiness** — LLM-based conversion is not lossless; information is compressed or dropped
2. **No RAG benchmark** — efficiency claims are anecdotal; no systematic comparison exists
3. **Scaling** — graph maintenance (edge consistency, orphan detection, contradiction resolution) gets non-trivial as the graph grows

These apply to all projects listed above, including this one. *[[llm-wiki-landscape]]*
