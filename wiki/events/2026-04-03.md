# Apr 3, 2026 — Two Meetings with Souti

Two sessions between [[sadra-sabouri|Sadra]] and [[souti-chattopadhyay|Souti]] covering creativity research and human-AI interaction patterns. Raw transcripts: [[Souti-3Apr2026-TRANSCRIPT-Part1|Part 1]], [[Souti-3Apr2026-TRANSCRIPT-Part2|Part 2]].

## Part 1: Creativity in Excel

Two distinct AI roles for creativity were articulated:
1. **Supporting human creativity** — AI as a tool that helps the human generate more/better ideas
2. **Closing the creativity gap** — AI as a substitute that compensates when the human lacks creative capacity

These are different design goals. The Torrance creativity test framework: divergent thinking (generating many ideas) → convergent thinking (selecting/refining). AI currently dominates convergent but weakens the divergent phase, connecting to [[productivity-creativity-tradeoff|the productivity-creativity tradeoff]].

Souti framed "creativity is the new productivity" as a potential paper angle — as LLMs commoditize productivity gains, creativity becomes the differentiating human capability.

## Part 2: Human-AI Interaction Patterns

[[human-as-api|Human-as-API]] framing introduced using Grice's maxims of cooperative communication — structuring agent requests to humans as well-specified API calls. The `interaction.md` concept: an agent learns user preferences from trajectory history.

[[gulf-of-envisioning|Gulf of Envisioning]] revisited: forward deployment engineering pattern proposed (deploy capability ahead of mental model, then train the mental model).

Persona simulation as a method for generating synthetic training data when real interaction data is scarce.
