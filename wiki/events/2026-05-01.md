# May 1, 2026 — Three-Way Sync: Wiki KB Project Takes Shape

First meeting with all three principals focused on the [[wiki-kb-project|Wiki KB Project]]. Raw transcript: [[T7-2026-05-01]].

**Attendees:** [[sadra-sabouri]], [[sumit-gulwani]], [[souti-chattopadhyay]]

## The Motivating Example

[[sumit-gulwani|Sumit]] opened by reading a real Teams channel exchange about a large problem X where teams were working in parallel without awareness of each other's approaches. Someone asked for a way to collate design documents across teams. Sumit's response in that channel: "we are considering an agentic workflow grounded in all our team's conversations." This made the abstract project concrete — the exact use case was happening in real time.

## Key Outcomes

**[[views|Views]] taxonomy defined:** design doc, contribution review, topic summary as example view types. Views can be hierarchical (sub-views as slide titles in a presentation view).

**[[workflows|Workflows]] as creative artifacts:** grounded theory analysis, multi-pass summarization (repeat 3×) recognized as non-obvious workflows worth explicitly capturing. Souti noted that models already know grounded theory — but workflows specify *when and how* to apply it, not just *that* it exists.

**Third contribution articulated:** efficiency via intermediate summaries — applying views to pre-computed summaries rather than raw sources, reducing latency and cost.

**[[digital-twins|Digital twins]] articulated** by [[souti-chattopadhyay|Souti]] as the long-term KB goal.

**Privacy concern raised** by Souti regarding [[semi-private-mashing|semi-private mashing]] — an important design constraint on what gets filtered into shared KBs.
