# May 2, 2026 — Two Meetings: Simulation Metaphor and KB Design

Two [[sadra-sabouri|Sadra]] + [[sumit-gulwani|Sumit]] meetings on the same day covering theory and KB design details. Also the day Sumit sent the [[Transcripts]] email with AI summaries and refined transcript. Raw transcripts: [[T8-2026-05-02-1|morning]], [[T9-2026-05-02-2|evening]].

## Morning Meeting

**[[simulation-metaphor|Simulation metaphor]] introduced** by Sadra: world progress = individual processing/choice-making + communication between people. KB accelerates both dimensions.

**Temporal dimension of KB** discussed: the KB is not a flat collection but a time-stamped evolution of ideas. Teams custom summary identified as a real-world analogue to [[views|Views]] — it is a predefined view applied to a single transcript.

**KB graph update strategies** discussed: when a new source arrives, how do you propagate changes? Sadra explored percolation approaches (changes ripple upward through dependent pages).

## Evening Meeting

**[[self-referential-kb|Self-referential presentation]]** idea formalized: the presentation on Thursday is about itself, generated from the KB that contains the transcripts of meetings about the KB.

**[[views|Views]] vs. [[workflows|Workflows]] distinction sharpened**: views are declarative (what output is desired); workflows are constructive (how to produce it). Multi-pass summarization is a workflow, not a view.

**[[purge-operation|Purge]] named** as a first-class KB operation — without it, contradictions accumulate over time.

**[[provenance-tracking|Provenance]] articulated**: "AI slop" (unverified AI output) vs. "AI beauty" (verified or high-quality). The KB must track which is which.

**Researcher agent as source type**: an autonomous AI researcher could itself produce raw sources for ingestion — blurring the line between human and AI contributors.
