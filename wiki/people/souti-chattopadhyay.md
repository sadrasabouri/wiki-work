# Souti Chattopadhyay

[[sadra-sabouri|Sadra]]'s PhD advisor at USC, referred to as "Rini." Brings the HCI and creativity research perspective to both projects.

## Intellectual Contributions

Coined [[digital-twins|digital twins]] as the ultimate KB goal in [[Sadra-1May2026-Transcript|the May 1 sync]]: capturing not just technical content but tacit knowledge, interaction styles, and emotional patterns.

Framed [[human-as-api|human-as-API]] using Grice's maxims of cooperative communication in [[Souti-3Apr2026-TRANSCRIPT-Part2|the Apr 3 Part 2 meeting]], giving the human-oversight problem a principled communication-theoretic foundation.

Was the first to note in [[GMT20260316-200648_Recording.transcript|the Mar 16 meeting]] that "the agent takes over control" — an early articulation of the [[steerability|steerability]] problem from the user's perspective, before Sumit named it.

Brought the creativity research angle: Torrance creativity tests, two AI roles (supporting human creativity vs. closing the creativity gap), and the framing of "creativity is the new productivity." See [[Souti-3Apr2026-TRANSCRIPT-Part1|Apr 3 Part 1]].

Raised privacy concerns about [[semi-private-mashing|semi-private mashing]] in [[Sadra-1May2026-Transcript|the May 1 sync]] — an important design constraint on the [[wiki-kb-project|Wiki KB Project]].

In [[Sadra-3May2026-TRANSCRIPT|the May 3 sync]], proposed the graphical interface vision: an embedded agent + wiki where users select files, chat with the agent, and define/modify views without touching the CLI. "Figuring out like what are the views that are going to be useful — that's the research contribution."
